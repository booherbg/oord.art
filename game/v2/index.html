Before You Go In

This is a short story told across five chapters, each about 15 minutes long. Four chapters follow the Wanderer. A fifth unlocks a new perspective entirely.

Read it the way you'd read a short story — one chapter at a time, at your own pace. There are no wrong answers.

When you finish a chapter, come back and choose the same character again. New choices will appear — look for the glowing options that unlock as you go deeper. Each run through the same evening reveals something different.

Music plays during this experience. You can adjust volume or mute at any time. To explore the lore and source material behind the story, visit the lore viewer.
