# Familiar Prose — Voice Audit
**Document reviewed: game/v2/prose/FAMILIAR_PROSE.md**
**Voice profile: ancient, clinical, intermittently comic. Perceives via frequency and density. Metaphors: mycelium, root systems, soil, tide.**

---

## Section: Before

**What's working:**
The strongest section. Every sentence establishes voice. "Appropriately jointed" is immediately iconic. "He does not know what security means in this context" is Pratchett-grade. The fog as the Familiar's idea because it "obscures the places where the architecture becomes unreliable" is perfect lore delivery — one sentence reveals both the cargo cult construction and the Familiar's practical intelligence. The self-correction ("Not a trap. A clinic.") establishes the Familiar's unreliable self-narration.

**Flags:**

**1. Human emotional justification leaking in:**
> "They are sick and do not know it. You are the treatment they did not seek. This is service. This is the work."

"They are sick" is clinical. Good. But "this is service" is the Familiar talking itself into ethical justification using human moral language. A being that perceives via frequency and density wouldn't frame harvesting as "service" — that's a human value judgment. **Fix:** "The system requires clearing. You are the clearing apparatus. This is function." Or just cut "This is service" and keep "This is the work."

**2. "the lure is humming" immediately contradicts the self-correction:**
> "The trap is set. The bait is good. The lure is humming. No. Not a trap."

This is great as a character moment — the Familiar's first instinct is predatory language, then it corrects to "clinic." But having three predatory terms in sequence ("trap," "bait," "lure") before the correction feels heavy. One would land harder. **Fix:** "The apparatus is ready. The lure is humming. No. Not a lure. A clinic."

## Section: The Opening

**What's working:**
"The first note is not a note. It is Sooboont exhaling through the aperture" is a perfect opening line. The rod going from inert to alive in a single pulse is good physical-functional description. The mycologist/soil metaphor for reading the room is exactly the right register.

**Flags:**

**1. Human psychological language:**
> "packed tight under layers of routine and obligation"

The Familiar perceives via frequency and density. It doesn't psychologize. "Routine and obligation" are human concepts — the Familiar would see these as calcification, as mineral layers, as density patterns. **Fix:** "packed tight under layers of calcification, compressed by years of surface living." Or better: "His Unity concentration is dense, packed into a deep seam. It will need work to reach the surface."

**2. Lore dump disguised as observation:**
> "The transcendentals exist in the spaces between people, not inside them. Unity is not something a single person possesses. It is something that occurs in the gap when two or more bodies synchronize."

This is a lecture. The Familiar KNOWS this — it doesn't need to remind itself. It reads like exposition addressed to the player, not internal observation. **Fix:** Show this through what the Familiar sees: "The rod responds to the gap between them, not to either body. It always responds to gaps." The principle is demonstrated, not stated.

**3. Wrong metaphor family:**
> "the connection arcs between them like a static discharge"

Static discharge is electrical/mechanical — Builder territory. The Familiar's metaphor family is organic and geological. **Fix:** "the connection threads between them like a root finding water."

## Section: The DJ OOR Slide

**What's working:**
"Your participation increases the density" is perfect — the Familiar as a node in its own network, not immune to the field it cultivates. The timber/grain metaphor for the paint-knuckle man is excellent. "Effectiveness and quality are the same thing in this context" is a great Familiar insight.

**Flags:**

**1. Mechanical process language too explicit:**
> "the mechanical process by which deposits are loosened"

The Familiar has organic metaphors. "Mechanical process" is Builder language. The Familiar would describe this as agitation in organic terms. **Fix:** "the agitation by which deposits are loosened from the substrate" — keeps the geological metaphor.

**2. The Slide section reads too much like a manual:**
> "Get them moving together. Dissolve the boundary between individual bodies. Create a unified field."

Three imperative sentences in a row. This reads like a procedure, not observation. The Familiar should be watching this happen, not reciting steps. **Fix:** "They are moving together. The boundaries are thinning. The field is forming." Same information, observation mode instead of instruction mode.

## Section: The Mating Ritual

**What's working:**
This is the Familiar's best section. "The way a fern unfurls. Slowly. Inevitably." is perfect. "Not target — source. Not source — deposit. The language is insufficient in every direction" is the best self-correction in the document. The pairing of sneaker-star woman and half-closed-eyes man, with the rod going quiet when the distance is right, is mechanically elegant and emotionally resonant. "You are wearing one. There is a difference." is the section's best line.

**Flags:**

**1. Telling what the Familiar sees instead of showing it:**
> "A capacity for attention so focused that it functions almost like a transcendental in itself."

This is analysis, not perception. The rod responded to this person — show the rod's response. What does "attention functioning like a transcendental" look like to a being that reads frequencies? **Fix:** "The rod responds to them. Not loudly — not the way it responded to the sneaker-star woman. But specifically. The frequency is unusual. Dense and focused, with almost no scattering. As if this person's attention itself is a deposit."

**2. Lore branch opportunity missed:**
The Familiar notes the Wanderer and moves past them. This is the right structural decision — "not yet" is good. But there's an opportunity for a lore branch here: [BRANCH POINT: Do you mark the Wanderer for future work, or leave them unmarked and let the mechanism decide?] This would create an A->B->C lore branch where B shows the Familiar's internal process of cataloguing potential subjects.

## Section: The Dance Circle

**What's working:**
"He dances the way a tree moves in wind — from the roots, through the trunk" is perfect. The beekeeper extended metaphor ("the bees do not know you are there, and the honey is not for them, and you are keeping them alive because alive bees make honey and that is the arrangement and it is old") is the document's crown jewel. Don't touch it.

**Flags:**

**1. Lore dump about the circle mechanism:**
> "The circle is an extraction point — a focused aperture within the larger aperture, a place where the membrane between planes is so thin that a single person standing in it becomes, briefly, a pure conduit."

This is the Familiar explaining the mechanism to the reader, not observing it. The Familiar already knows what the circle is — it wouldn't narrate the definition. **Fix:** Show the circle through the Familiar's frequency perception: "The circle glows. Not visually — not in a way the humans would see. The membrane is thinnest here. A single body standing in it becomes a conduit so pure the rod sings from across the room." Moves from expository to observational.

**2. Naming transcendentals breaks the clinical voice:**
> "She is yielding Truth. Unselfconscious presence, extracted in real time, flowing through the aperture and down into the membrane"

The Familiar is labeling the experience for the reader. "Yielding Truth" is shorthand — the Familiar would perceive this as a specific frequency pattern or density change, not a named category. **Fix:** "She is yielding. The flow through her body is visible to you — luminous, dense, the specific signature you have learned to associate with unmediated presence. The aperture draws it down."

**3. "This is not surveillance. This is care" — tone break:**
> "This is not surveillance. This is care."

"Care" is human emotional language. The Familiar's version of care is functional — keeping the bees alive because alive bees make honey. The beekeeper passage does this perfectly. This earlier sentence is too direct, too human. **Fix:** Cut it. The beekeeper passage that follows does all the work this sentence is trying to do, and does it better.

## Section: The Closing

**What's working:**
"the way soil receives water" is the right metaphor. "the way a wound seals, the way a fracture knits" — good healing language. The dissolution ending is structurally brilliant — the prose degrading as the body becomes optional. "A jacket that says SECURITY is folded neatly on a chair. There is no one inside it. There is no evidence that there ever was." is a perfect final image.

**Flags:**

**1. Dissolution rhythm becomes formulaic:**
> "Your hands are — where are your hands? ... what are your eyes doing?"

The fragmentation pattern is: sentence fragment, question, fragment, question. It's effective the first time but by the third iteration the rhythm is predictable. Vary the breakdown. **Fix:** Mix in some statements that are wrong rather than questions: "Your hands are on the door. Your hands are in your coat. Your hands are — you don't have a coat. You don't have hands. You have a function and the function is complete."

**2. Time-reference inconsistency:**
> "for — how long? Time is not your strongest suit."

This appears in the "Before" section about the transcendental flow being blocked. It's a good self-aware joke. But the Familiar references time casually elsewhere ("You have stood in forests older than language") without similar disclaimers. Either the Familiar is uncertain about time everywhere, or it's selectively uncertain. Pick one. The selective uncertainty is funnier — keep it where it creates comedy, cut where it's just a tic.

## Cross-Character Threading Assessment

**Strong threads:**
- Paint-knuckle man: Wanderer sees paint. Familiar sees Unity concentration packed like grain in timber. Different literacy, same person. Clean.
- Sneaker-star woman: Wanderer sees pen mark. Familiar sees Truth concentration, "human sigil she probably doesn't know she made." Excellent elevation.
- Bartender/bouncer: Wanderer accepts drinks and walks past security without question. Familiar built both roles. "The seam holds." Perfect dramatic irony.
- Jacket woman: Wanderer sees someone who won't take off their jacket. Familiar sees maintained boundary, armor. Good.

**Missing or weak threads:**
- The Wanderer: The Familiar notes them as "unusual," but the description is vague ("a capacity for attention so focused"). What does the rod actually DO when it passes the Wanderer? The Wanderer felt a hum in their fillings (w_007e). The Familiar should describe what the rod did — the specific harmonic signature it read. This is the key cross-character payoff moment.
- DJ OOR: The Familiar describes OOR as a reactor/construct. Good. But the Familiar's emotional relationship to OOR is underwritten — is there respect? Fear? The functional affection of a field technician for the machine they service? The "Before" section establishes professionalism, but the Closing doesn't revisit it. How does the Familiar feel about OOR when the portal closes?

## Humor Assessment

**Landing:**
- "appropriately jointed" — yes
- "He does not know what security means in this context" — yes
- "The glasses are mostly correct" — yes
- "she is pouring liquids into glasses with the careful focus of someone performing a ritual she learned from observation" — yes, and it compounds with "approximate accuracy" later
- "Effectiveness and quality are the same thing in this context" — dry, good

**Not landing:**
- "Not a trap. A clinic." — The comedy of the self-correction works. But "clinic" isn't quite funny enough. "Clinic" implies competence. The comedy is in the gap between the Familiar's self-image (healer) and its actual function (harvester). A funnier replacement might lean harder: "Not a trap. A... treatment facility. Involuntary, admittedly. But the treatment is real."

**Missing opportunities:**
The Familiar's humor drops out almost entirely in the Mating Ritual and Dance Circle sections — the two most operationally intense moments. This is where the Pratchett DNA should be strongest. Ancient being doing delicate work, correcting itself, noting absurdities. One or two dry observations during the pairing sequence would prevent the section from becoming too solemn.

## Summary

**Strongest section:** Before. Every line earns its place.

**Weakest section:** The DJ OOR Slide. Too manual-like, wrong metaphor family in places.

**Recurring issues:**
- Lore dumps where observation would serve better (Slide, Dance Circle)
- Human emotional language breaking through ("service," "care," "routine and obligation")
- One wrong-family metaphor (static discharge)
- Dissolution rhythm needs variation

**What must not be cut:**
- "appropriately jointed" and all body-as-tool references
- The beekeeper passage
- "Not target — source. Not source — deposit. The language is insufficient in every direction."
- "You are wearing one. There is a difference."
- "A jacket that says SECURITY is folded neatly on a chair."
- The fog as the Familiar's idea
