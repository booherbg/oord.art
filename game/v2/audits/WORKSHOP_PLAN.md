## Core Thesis
The current Run 3 arc awards "goodness" but delivers "truth take two." Both Run 2 and Run 3 use the same structure: strip away a coping mechanism → arrive at raw presence → connect. Run 2 strips autopilot (body). Run 3 strips the narrator (mind). Same movement, different layer.

Goodness requires **agency**. Not something taken from you — something you choose to give. The Wanderer should DO something for someone, not just have an internal shift.

**Revised arc:** The Wanderer sees the jacket woman holding herself apart and CHOOSES to approach her. The narrator goes quiet not from revelation but because genuine care for another person leaves no bandwidth for self-narration. The silence is a side effect of goodness, not the goal.

## 2 Variations
**A. The Offering** — Goodness as sacrifice. The Wanderer tries to give the matchbook to the jacket woman. It comes back. The system won't let you be truly selfless, but the act of trying was genuine. Darker take.

**B. The Regulars** — Goodness as communal care. The loading dock crew becomes central. Accumulated small kindnesses (door-holding, including a stranger, noting who needs help). The Wanderer crosses the floor because nobody else went this time.
