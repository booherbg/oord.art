## Section: Before — 2 edits

### Edit 1: Trim the predatory language before the self-correction
**Impact:** Low. Three predatory terms in sequence before the correction is heavy. One is funnier.

**BEFORE:**
```
The trap is set. The bait is good. The lure is humming.

No. Not a trap. You correct yourself. Not a trap. A clinic. A healing apparatus. They are sick and do not know it. You are the treatment they did not seek. This is service. This is the work.
```

**AFTER:**
```
The lure is humming.

No. Not a lure. You correct yourself. A clinic. A healing apparatus. They are sick and do not know it. You are the treatment they did not seek. This is the work.
```

**Changes:** (1) "The trap is set. The bait is good." → cut. One predatory term ("lure") is enough to trigger the self-correction. Three makes the Familiar sound like it's wallowing in the metaphor before correcting. (2) "Not a trap. A clinic." → "Not a lure. A clinic." Matches the correction to what was actually said. (3) Cut "This is service." — human moral language. "This is the work" is the Familiar's version of the same sentiment and it's already there.

---

### Edit 2: No changes to the rest of "Before"
The "before" section is otherwise the strongest writing in the document. "Appropriately jointed," the bouncer/bartender descriptions, the fog, OOR as reactor — all clean. Leave them.

## Section: The Opening — 2 edits

### Edit 3: Replace human psychological language with Familiar perception
**Impact:** Medium. "Routine and obligation" are human concepts. The Familiar reads frequency and density, not psychology.

**BEFORE:**
```
His Unity concentration is dense, packed tight under layers of routine and obligation. He is a good deposit. He will yield well.
```

**AFTER:**
```
His Unity concentration is dense, packed into a deep seam under years of calcification. He is a good deposit. He will yield well.
```

**Changes:** "routine and obligation" → "years of calcification." Stays in the geological metaphor family. "Deep seam" is mining language — consistent with the Familiar's perception of humans as deposits.

---

### Edit 4: Replace lore lecture with observation
**Impact:** High. Three sentences of exposition about how transcendentals work, addressed to the reader, not to the Familiar's own process.

**BEFORE:**
```
The transcendentals exist in the spaces between people, not inside them. Unity is not something a single person possesses. It is something that occurs in the gap when two or more bodies synchronize. Your job is to close the gaps.
```

**AFTER:**
```
The rod responds to the gaps between bodies, not to the bodies themselves. It always has. Your job is to close the gaps.
```

**Changes:** Three sentences of cosmological exposition → one sentence of rod behavior. Same information, delivered as observation of the Familiar's own instrument rather than a lecture about metaphysics. "It always has" carries the weight of deep time without a lore dump. Keeps "Your job is to close the gaps" — that's clean.

## Section: The Mating Ritual — 2 edits

### Edit 8: Show the rod's response to the Wanderer instead of analyzing it
**Impact:** Medium. "A capacity for attention so focused that it functions almost like a transcendental in itself" is analysis, not perception.

**BEFORE:**
```
There is something in this person that is unusual. A capacity for attention so focused that it functions almost like a transcendental in itself. They notice things. They notice you. You feel their gaze on your antlers and it is not fear and it is not amusement. It is recognition. Not of what you are. Of the fact that you are something.
```

**AFTER:**
```
There is something in this person that is unusual. The rod's response is dense and focused, almost no scattering — a frequency you don't often encounter. They notice things. They notice you. You feel their gaze on your antlers and it is not fear and it is not amusement. It is recognition. Not of what you are. Of the fact that you are something.
```

**Changes:** Replaced the abstract analysis ("capacity for attention...functions almost like a transcendental") with what the Familiar actually perceives through the rod — a dense, focused signal with no scattering. Shows the same information through the Familiar's instrument rather than through interpretation. Keeps the payoff sentences ("They notice you," "recognition") untouched — those are good.

## Section: The Dance Circle — 3 edits

### Edit 10: Replace lore dump about the circle with observation
**Impact:** High. The Familiar is explaining the game's mechanism to the reader.

**BEFORE:**
```
You understand this mechanism better than most of the rituals because it is the most direct. The circle is an extraction point — a focused aperture within the larger aperture, a place where the membrane between planes is so thin that a single person standing in it becomes, briefly, a pure conduit. The arrow is DJ OOR's selection tool. It identifies the deposits that are closest to full yield and isolates them.
```

**AFTER:**
```
The circle glows. Not visually — not in a way the humans would see. The membrane is thinnest here. A single body standing in it becomes a conduit so pure the rod sings from across the room. The arrow spins. It is not random. It is an assay — identifying the deposits closest to full yield.
```

**Changes:** Cut the self-explaining frame ("You understand this mechanism better than most...because it is the most direct"). The Familiar doesn't need to tell itself what it knows. Replaced the definition-style exposition with what the Familiar SEES — the glow, the thinness, the rod singing. "Assay" is geological vocabulary — better than "selection tool" for the Familiar's register. Kept the essential information (focused aperture, identifies deposits) but delivered through perception rather than explanation.

### Edit 11: Show the transcendental flow, don't name it
**Impact:** Medium.

**BEFORE:**
```
She is yielding Truth. Unselfconscious presence, extracted in real time, flowing through the aperture and down into the membrane and through to Sooboont. You feel the pull increase. Sooboont is drawing. The drain is clearing.

**AFTER:**
```
She is yielding. The flow through her is visible to you — luminous, dense, the specific signature you have learned to associate with unmediated presence. It moves through the aperture and down through the membrane. You feel Sooboont draw. The drain is clearing.

**Changes:** (1) "yielding Truth" → "yielding." Don't name the transcendental — the Familiar perceives the frequency pattern, not the label. (2) "Unselfconscious presence, extracted in real time" → "luminous, dense, the specific signature you have learned to associate with unmediated presence." Shows what the Familiar sees through its frequency-perception, with "unmediated presence" as the Familiar's functional description rather than a category name. (3) "Sooboont is drawing" → "Sooboont draw" — tighter.

## Section: The Closing — 1 edit

### Edit 13: Vary the dissolution rhythm
**Impact:** Medium. The fragmentation currently alternates fragment → question → fragment → question. Predictable by the third iteration.

**BEFORE:**
```
The alley is dark. The sodium lights are off or were never on or are on but you cannot see them because your eyes are — what are your eyes doing? Your hands are — where are your hands?
```

**AFTER:**
```
The alley is dark. The sodium lights are off or were never on or are on but you cannot see them because your eyes are — your eyes are fine. Your eyes are not your eyes. Your hands are at your sides. Your hands are not at your sides. Your hands —
```

**Changes:** Instead of two questions (interrogating the body's absence), the dissolution now contradicts itself. "Your eyes are fine" / "Your eyes are not your eyes" — the Familiar is losing the ability to report reliably. Statements that are wrong are more disorienting than questions. The dash at the end of "Your hands —" trails into the next paragraph's "The body is optional" without the neat closure of a question mark.

---

### No changes to the rest of the Closing
"The way soil receives water," "the way a wound seals, the way a fracture knits," the dissolution run-on sentence, "A jacket that says SECURITY is folded neatly on a chair" — all clean. The dissolution sequence from "The body is optional" through "you are —" is the best continuous writing in the project. Don't touch it.
