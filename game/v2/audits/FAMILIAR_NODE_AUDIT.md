## Flagged Nodes — Specific Fixes

### f_003 — THE APERTURE

**Issue 1: Lore exposition.** The Familiar is explaining WHY the harvest exists in psychological terms rather than thinking operationally.

**Current:**
> The flow has been blocked long enough that the humans mistake numbness for contentment. The drain must be cleared or the basin overflows.

**Proposed:**
> The flow has been blocked. The material world has calcified. The drain must be cleared.

Cut the psychological interpretation. The Familiar doesn't analyze the human condition — it notes operational status (clogged, needs clearing).

**Issue 2:** "Not a lure. A clinic. They are sick and do not know it." — This self-correction is excellent. It shows the Familiar's uncertainty about its own role and the ethical framing of the work. Keep.

## Missing Elements — Threading Humor and Body Into the Middle

The Familiar's voice has two features that are strong in the opening (f_001–f_003) and closing (f_018–f_020) but fade during the operational middle (f_006–f_014):

### 1. The Humor

The dry, Pratchett-adjacent bemusement at human ritual disappears during the mating ritual. The Familiar is an ancient being wearing a body at a rave — there should be at least some quiet amusement threading through.

**Suggested additions:**

**f_007 (THE SLIDE):** After "But the principle is always the same" — add:
> The humans have a name for this particular sequence. You have heard it four hundred times across as many harvests. You do not retain the name. You retain the function.

**f_012 (THE SETTLING):** Add "The seam holds." (noted above). This is humorous AND structural.

### 2. The Body-as-Tool

References to the body as a borrowed instrument are foregrounded in f_001 ("appropriately jointed," "it has hands") and f_020 ("the body is optional"), but the Familiar forgets about the body during the operational middle. When the Familiar is walking through a crowd, taking someone's hand, adjusting positions — these are all acts performed WITH the body. The tool-awareness should persist.

**Suggested addition:**

**f_010a (THE PAIRING):** After "You take her hand. Yours is warm. Hers is warmer." — add:
> The hand does what you need it to do.

One beat. Maintains the Familiar's peculiar relationship with its own borrowed body.
